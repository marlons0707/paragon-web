The Impact Of BPR On Organizational Performance

done_all The two cornerstones of any organization are the people and the processes.

done_all If individuals are motivated and working hard, yet the business processes are cumbersome and non-essential activities remain, organizational performance will be poor.

done_all Business Process Reengineering is the key to transforming how people work.

done_all What appear to be minor changes in processes can have dramatic effects on cash flow, service delivery and customer satisfaction.

done_all Even the act of documenting business processes alone will typically improve organizational efficiency by 10%.
